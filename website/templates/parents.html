Winter After School Program

Tuesdays January 5, 12, 19, 2021 via Zoom

The Winter After School Program is a recurring event on Tuesdays at 3:15pm during the winter! The After School Program is designed for children grades 1-6 from schools in the Davis and Sacramento Area. We are planning for this to be a socializing event for students where we encourage communication and positivity. We will be hosting fun and engaging activities such as arts & crafts, watching kids' videos, and playing games such as online Pictionary (skribbl.io). There will be different activities each week!
